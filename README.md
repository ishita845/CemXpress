The proposed solution is a web-based quick-commerce platform designed exclusively for cement and core construction materials. The platform will have three main modules: Buyer, Seller, and Delivery.

Buyers (contractors, builders, homeowners) can search for materials, compare prices from nearby suppliers, place orders, choose delivery times, and track deliveries in real time. Sellers (dealers and warehouses) can manage inventory, set prices, and process incoming orders through a digital dashboard. Delivery drivers can receive assignments, navigate to pickup and drop-off locations, and update delivery status.

This system will use location-based supplier matching and optimized logistics to ensure rapid and reliable material delivery, helping reduce project delays and improve supply chain efficiency.
